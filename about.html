About PetFarm

Rescuing Pets • Building Families • Delivering Love Worldwide

Who We Are

PetFarm is more than just a pet adoption platform — we are a global network of animal lovers committed to giving pets a second chance at life. With agencies across France, the United States, Germany, and Belgium, we have built a trusted international presence dedicated to rescuing, caring for, and rehoming pets.

Through strong partnerships with ethical breeders and rescue centers, PetFarm ensures that every animal receives proper care, medical attention, and love before finding a new home. Our mission is simple: connect happy pets with loving families, no matter where they are in the world.

What We Do

Pet Rescue

We rescue abandoned and vulnerable animals and give them new hope.

Trusted Breeders

We partner with responsible breeders who prioritize animal welfare.

Global Network

Agencies across Europe and the USA ensure smooth adoption worldwide.

Safe & Reliable Delivery

At PetFarm, we understand that bringing a new pet home is a special moment. That’s why we provide secure and stress-free delivery services. Whether you are in Europe or abroad, our trained team ensures that every pet travels safely, comfortably, and arrives happy and healthy at your doorstep.
